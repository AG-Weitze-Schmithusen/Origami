  [33X[0;0Y[13X  Important premise: We assume in this section, that all considered origamis
  have   no   nontrivial   translations.   This  is  equivalent  to  that  the
  corresponding torus cover has no nontrivial deck transformations.[113X[133X
  
  
  [1X3.1 [33X[0;0YThe non tautological part of homology[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThis  section  contains functions, which compute a basis of the homology [22XH =
  H_1( mathcalO, Z)[122X of an origami [22XmathcalO[122X with [22Xd[122X squares and the action of an
  affine  transformation [22Xf[122X of [22XmathcalO[122X on [22XH[122X. The later is the map [22Xf_âˆ—: H -> H[122X.
  We  consider  the  cellular  homology  with the squares [22XQ_1, dots Q_d[122X of the
  origami  as  2-cells.  We call [22XÏƒ_i[122X the bottom edge of the square [22XQ_i[122X and [22XÎ¾_i[122X
  the  left edge of the square [22XQ_i[122X. Then we can represent an element of [22XH[122X as a
  formal  linear  combination  of  the  [22XÏƒ 's[122X and the [22XÎ¾ 's[122X. In [5XGAP[105X we represent
  elements  of  [22XH[122X as elements of [22XZ^2d[122X in the following way: [22XÏƒ_i[122X is represented
  by  [22Xe_i[122X  and  [22XÎ¾_i[122X is represented by [22Xe_i + d[122X, where [22Xe_i[122X is the i-th. standard
  basis  vector  of  [22XZ^2d[122X. Formal sums of elements in [22XH[122X are represented by the
  appropriate  sums  in [22XZ^2d[122X. One has to keep in mind that this description is
  not  unique. More precisely the homology [22XH[122X is isomorphic to a subquotient of
  [22XZ^2d[122X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  homology  has  a  splitting as sum of the [13Xtautological part[113X [22XH^T[122X and the
  [13Xnon-tautological  part  [113X  [22XH^NT[122X  which  is equivalent under the action of the
  affine group Aff[22X(mathcalO)[122X of the origami [22XmathcalO[122X. I.e. [22XH = H^T âŠ• H^NT[122X with
  [22XH^T[122X  and  [22XH^NT[122X  both invariant under the action of Aff[22X(mathcalO)[122X. The sum is
  orthogonal  with respect to the intersection form, the dimension of [22XH^T[122X is 2
  and  the  dimension of [22XH^NT[122X is [22X2g-2[122X. In the part of the package described in
  this  chapter  we  provide  functions which compute the action of the affine
  group  on  the  homology  [22XH[122X  and  on the non-tautological part [22XH^NT[122X and thus
  obtain  the  [13X shadow Veech group[113X. For more information on this topic see for
  example [BKK+22].[133X
